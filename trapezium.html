Trapezium (Trapezoid)

At least one pair of parallel sides.

Properties

  • One pair of parallel sides
  • Angles on each leg are supplementary

Formulas

  • Area = (a + b) / 2 × height
  • Perimeter = sum of sides

Fun Fact

Region note: US name is trapezoid; in some places it's trapezium.
