Rectangle

Opposite sides equal; all internal angles are 90°.

Properties

  • Opposite sides equal
  • All angles 90°
  • Diagonals are equal

Formulas

  • Area = width × height
  • Perimeter = 2 × (width + height)

Fun Fact

A rectangle with all sides equal becomes a square.
