Square

A square has all sides equal and every angle is 90°.

Properties

  • All sides equal
  • All angles 90°
  • Diagonals bisect each other at 90° and are equal

Formulas

  • Area = side²
  • Perimeter = 4 × side

Fun Fact

Every square is a rectangle, but not every rectangle is a square!
