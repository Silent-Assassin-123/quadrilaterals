Rhombus

All sides equal; opposite angles equal.

Properties

  • All sides equal
  • Opposite angles equal
  • Diagonals cross at right angles and bisect angles

Formulas

  • Area = (d₁ × d₂) / 2
  • Perimeter = 4 × side

Fun Fact

A rotated square is still a rhombus.
