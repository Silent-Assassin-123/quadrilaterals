Kite

Two pairs of adjacent equal sides.

Properties

  • Two pairs of adjacent equal sides
  • One diagonal bisects the other
  • One pair of opposite angles equal

Formulas

  • Area = (d₁ × d₂) / 2
  • Perimeter = 2 × (a + b)

Fun Fact

Kites were historically used for flight experiments and signalling.
