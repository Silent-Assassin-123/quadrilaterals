Parallelogram

Opposite sides are parallel and equal in length.

Properties

  • Opposite sides equal
  • Opposite angles equal
  • Diagonals bisect each other

Formulas

  • Area = base × height
  • Perimeter = 2 × (a + b)

Fun Fact

A rectangle is a parallelogram with right angles.
